About Me

I grew up in Tennessee and got a Bachelors degree in Concrete Industry Management at Middle Tennessee State University. After graduation, I found an interest in coding and thought about how amazing it would be to create something that would have a significant positive impact and that I can be proud of.

I took the time to learn front end web development after I graduated from college. I'd spend hours each day watching modules and doing challenges, which eventually helped me land my first job as a Wordpress developer for a digital marketing agency in Nashville!

I love to take photos with my Nikon, collect comic books, and make all sorts of art in my spare time, but these days i'm actually making more efforts to expand my knowledge on coding. I Take great interest in learning Javascript, especially. Once I've learned enough, I plan to study more of Reactjs and React Native as part of my plan to be a full-stack developer.
